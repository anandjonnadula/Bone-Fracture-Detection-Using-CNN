Project Abstract

This academic project presents a Deep Learning application utilizing Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to automate bone fracture detection from X-ray images. The primary objective is to deliver rapid, high-precision fracture classification to assist orthopedic diagnostics and streamline patient care.
